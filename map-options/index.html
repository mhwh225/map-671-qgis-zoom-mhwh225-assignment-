Question :Which parts of the Country are most likely to be affected by fire?

Geoprocessing Analysis Firstly I Took the fire point data from the earlier activity and joined it with a US county map. After that I sorted the counties by number of fires that occured it them. This provided the map shown above where we can see the concentration of fires in california and arizona.
